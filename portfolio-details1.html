Project detail

In this project, I was grouped into a team of 4. We were given the product details of that warehouse, it was supposed to replace an old system that included using Microsoft Excel and handwritten information. The new system we got in the product details contained all of the information including a detailed explanation about the users of the system, a technical overview of the majority of screens in the software, the system also has BATCH processes and Query option that connect to a database.
At the end of the project, we handed our STP projects, test cases in the TestRail and presented the presentation in front of an audience.
